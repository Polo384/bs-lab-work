UTMB

The UTMB (Ultra-Trail du Mont-Blanc) is a prestigious trail running event that challenges participants to traverse the rugged terrain of the Mont-Blanc massif, testing their endurance and determination.

After completing this challenge, you will have conquered one of the most demanding trail running events, showcasing your endurance and determination.

Requirement

There are some requirements to join the UTMB event:

  • You are finisher of a 100 miles race
  • You are finisher of a UTMB World Series race
  • You are motivated to push your limits and achieve greatness

Race details

There are some stats about the UTMB event:

  • Distance: 109 miles
  • Elevation gain: 32 000 ft
  • Maximum allowed time: 46h30
  • Start : Chamonix-Mont-Blanc

Best competitors

Learn more about the legends of UTMB

Kilian Jornet

Kilian Jornet is a renowned trail runner known for his multiple victories at the UTMB and holds the record for the fastest ascent of Mont Blanc.

Courtney Dauwalter

Courtney Dauwalter is an exceptional trail runner with multiple wins at the UTMB, known for her endurance and resilience.

François d'Haene

François d'Haene is a legendary trail runner with multiple UTMB victories, known for his strength and strategy on ultra-distance.

Alexandre Boucheix

Alexandre Boucheix, also known as "Casquette Verte", is a figure in the trail running community, celebrated for his achievements and unique style.
